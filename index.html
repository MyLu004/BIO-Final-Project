Reaction Time Tester

Your session consists of 12 rounds divided into four levels of distraction:
Level 0: Rounds 1-3 (No distraction)
Level 1: Rounds 4-6 (Low distraction)
Level 2: Rounds 7-9 (Moderate distraction with target movement)
Level 3: Rounds 10-12 (High distraction with rapid target movement)
